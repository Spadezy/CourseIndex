Assignment 2: Parametrics and Generation!

For this assignment, we were tasked with developing a daily practice of "code sketches." Rather than constructing a single project, our goal was to allot daily time in to exploring generative art.

With our assigned letter as our inspiration, we were to create 21 code “sketches” of that letter in p5 over the course of 3 weeks.

The letter I chose was "T"

Most of the sketches were created with code that were to create lines. I used mostly javaScript to implement everything. Some were made with a mousePressed function others were made with a draw function. Over the assignment, these were the 21 unique sketches I created.
